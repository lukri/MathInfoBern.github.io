WhoWe Are

We are the student association of all Mathematics and Computer Science students at the University of Bern. Our foundation is independent from university and entirely based on voluntary work by students with the primary focus on promoting an active exchange between students to ease their studies. If you too study one of these disciplines we warmly welcome you to be part of our meetings and events and make use of our extensive repository of resources such as exams or summaries!

On a regular base we organize casual events such as movienights, table football tournaments culminating in our big semester-end-party - the ideal event to make new connections within ExWi. Our events with our sponsors enable you to get a glimpse of the business world through engaging discussions with insiders and interesting presentations.

Resources

We are currently in the process of setting up our archive consisting of a wide range of old exams and other helpful material for your study. While we are not able to cover every course, we continuously extend our collection to keep it as current and comprehensive as possible.
